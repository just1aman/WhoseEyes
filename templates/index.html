How it works

  1. Join — Everyone enters the room with the same 6-letter code.
  2. Upload — Crop and upload a close-up photo of just your eyes.
  3. Guess — The host starts the round. Match each pair of eyes to the right person. Faster correct guesses earn more points.
  4. Score — The leaderboard updates after everyone has guessed.
